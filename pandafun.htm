What Are Pandas?

While the word "panda" often brings to mind a large furry white beast, people who talk about pandas are often only refering to Giant Panda. It's lesser known friend, the Red Panda, also falls under the panda family, however and both are vital animals in the rich, diverse ecosystems of our world.
